Phase 1

In the following screens we will offer a number of different bets to you.

Betting always costs 1 credit. If you win a bet, you win 1 more credit.

If you don't bet you don't lose anything (but you can't win either).

For each bet, you can ask a question to the sender, then decide if/how you want to bet.

Keep in mind that the sender wants to help you: bets and winnings are split between the two of you.

You won't get any feedback during the game, but we'll keep track of your performance and give feedback at the end.
